Woocommerce Advance Report plugin shows you all sales and order  information in one report dashboard in very easy to understand graph view format .

== Description ==

Features:-
1) Display today’s total earnings by date.
2) Displays Top Products and Top Five Category.
3) Shows the visitor count.
4) Shows the number of Coupons and Payment Gateway.
5) Display total amount of refund and count number of it.
6) Shows total amount of sales, taxt and average sales per order.
7) It display count of total product ,order and total register customers.
8) It graphically present:
	1) This month sales by date,
	2) Weekly gross,
	3) last two month gross,
	4) Last six month gross,
	5) Order count by country,
	6) Top five ctegories order count,
	7) Payment gateway and
	8) Existing-New customer.
